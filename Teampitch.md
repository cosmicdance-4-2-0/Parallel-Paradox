Core Concept

Most game AI falls into one of two extremes: rigid scripted behavior or opaque black-box systems that are hard to tune. Our goal is a middle path: modular cognition, where each lens governs a domain of behavior.

The Human lens provides social believability, personality, and narrative grounding.

The Predictive lens handles foresight‚Äîanticipation of player actions, tactical planning, and probabilistic reasoning.

The Systemic lens integrates agents with global mechanics: economies, resources, logistics, and faction structures.

The Harmonic lens is the stabilizer, monitoring and adjusting the other lenses to prevent extremes‚Äîrunaway aggression, faction collapse, or immersion-breaking omniscience.


This creates agents that feel intelligent without cheating, challenging without frustrating, and adaptive without becoming unpredictable noise.

Design Benefits

1. Modularity

Each lens can be developed, tested, optimized, and updated independently. This lets design iterate rapidly without destabilizing the entire system.

2. Scalability

Lightweight agents can run with only one or two lenses active; major factions or boss-level NPCs can use the full suite.

3. Stability Through Feedback

The harmonic layer prevents catastrophic failures such as economic collapse, exponential faction war, or unbeatable predictive AI.

High-Level Architecture

Perception ‚Üí Lens Evaluation (parallel) ‚Üí Lens Fusion ‚Üí Action ‚Üí Harmonic Feedback

Each lens evaluates the current game state from its own perspective.

The Harmonic layer fuses these evaluations into a single action plan, adjusts lens weights, and records long-term trends.

This architecture is deliberately lightweight: it scales downward to indie-friendly workloads and upward to large-world simulations.

Use Cases

Companion AI

Human lens gives personality.

Predictive lens anticipates player needs in combat or exploration.

Systemic lens manages inventory, cooldowns, and positioning.

Harmonic lens balances autonomy to avoid annoyance.


Faction Simulation

Diplomacy, morale, and culture emerge from the Human lens.

Military and economic forecasting use the Predictive lens.

Infrastructure and trade networks rely on the Systemic lens.

The Harmonic lens prevents steamrolled maps or dead-world stagnation.


Open-World Populations

The system creates ecosystems and societies that evolve with the player, not despite the player.
